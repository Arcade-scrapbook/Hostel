Admission Criteria:

  • Admissions are given to the students based on merit and social reservation norms notified by the Government of Maharashtra.
  • Hostel Facility is not available for students admitted in the Direct Second Year Diploma Programme.
  • Students living in the area of Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation are not eligible for hostel admission.
  • Students who want hostel facilities should have cleared all subjects in the previous year.
